# Death and Dying

When a creature drops to 0 Hit Points, it either dies immediately or falls unconscious.

## Instant Death

The main ways a creature dies instantly are:

- Monster death:
  - a monster normally dies the instant it drops to `0` Hit Points
  - the GM can choose to ignore this for an individual monster
- Hit Point maximum reduced to `0`:
  - if a creature's Hit Point maximum reaches `0`, it dies
- Massive damage:
  - when damage reduces a character to `0` Hit Points and damage remains, the character dies if the remainder equals or exceeds Hit Point maximum

## Falling Unconscious

If a character reaches 0 Hit Points and does not die instantly:

- it gains the Unconscious condition
- it begins making Death Saving Throws

## Death Saving Throws

Whenever a creature starts its turn with `0` Hit Points, it makes a Death Saving Throw.

Rules:

- roll `1d20`
- `10` or higher is a success
- `9` or lower is a failure
- on the third success, the creature becomes Stable
- on the third failure, the creature dies
- successes and failures do not need to be consecutive
- the count resets when the creature regains any Hit Points or becomes Stable

Special results:

- natural `1`: counts as two failures
- natural `20`: the creature regains `1` Hit Point

## Damage at 0 Hit Points

- any damage while at `0` Hit Points causes one failed Death Saving Throw
- damage from a Critical Hit causes two failed Death Saving Throws instead
- if the damage equals or exceeds Hit Point maximum, the creature dies

## Stabilizing a Character

A creature can take the Help action to try to stabilize a creature with `0` Hit Points by succeeding on a `DC 10 Wisdom (Medicine)` check.

A Stable creature:

- does not make Death Saving Throws
- still has the Unconscious condition
- becomes unstable again if it takes damage
- regains `1` Hit Point after `1d4` hours if not healed sooner

## Character Demise

If a player character dies, revival magic can sometimes restore that character. Otherwise, the player may need to create a new character to join the group.
