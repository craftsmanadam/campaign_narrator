# Ability Checks

Ability checks are used when a creature attempts something meaningful and uncertain that is not an attack.

## Ability Families

- Strength: lift, push, pull, break
- Dexterity: move quickly, nimbly, or quietly
- Constitution: endure strain
- Intelligence: reason or remember
- Wisdom: notice details or behavior
- Charisma: influence, entertain, deceive

## Proficiency with Checks

Add Proficiency Bonus when:

- a relevant skill applies and the creature has that proficiency, or
- a relevant tool applies and the creature has that proficiency.

The GM determines which proficiency is relevant.

## Typical DCs

- Very easy: `5`
- Easy: `10`
- Medium: `15`
- Hard: `20`
- Very hard: `25`
- Nearly impossible: `30`

## Passive Perception

Passive Perception is:

`10 + Wisdom (Perception) modifier`

Use it when awareness is being judged without an active roll.
