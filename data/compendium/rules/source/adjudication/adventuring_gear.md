# Adventuring Gear

Adventuring gear contains many of the items most likely to affect play directly. The SRD defines their price, weight, and specific rules text where needed.

## Important Item Rules

### Acid

When you take the Attack action, you can replace one of your attacks with throwing a vial of Acid at a creature or object within `20 feet`. The target makes a Dexterity save with DC `8 + Dexterity modifier + Proficiency Bonus` or takes `2d6 Acid` damage.

### Alchemist's Fire

When you take the Attack action, you can replace one of your attacks with throwing Alchemist's Fire at a creature or object within `20 feet`. The target makes the same Dexterity save formula or takes `1d4 Fire` damage and starts burning.

### Basic Poison

As a Bonus Action, coat one weapon or up to three pieces of ammunition. A creature that takes Piercing or Slashing damage from it takes an extra `1d4 Poison` damage. The poison lasts `1 minute` or until its damage is dealt.

### Potion of Healing

Potion of Healing is a magic item. As a Bonus Action, you can drink it or administer it to another creature within `5 feet`. The drinker regains `2d4 + 2` Hit Points.

### Rope

As a Utilize action, tie a knot with a successful `DC 10 Dexterity (Sleight of Hand)` check. Bursting the rope requires a successful `DC 20 Strength (Athletics)` check. Binding an unwilling creature requires the target to already be Grappled, Incapacitated, or Restrained.

### Portable Ram

Using a Portable Ram to break down a door grants `+4` to the Strength check. One other character can help, granting Advantage.

### Torch

A Torch burns for `1 hour`, casting Bright Light in a `20-foot` radius and Dim Light for another `20 feet`. It can also be used as a Simple Melee weapon that deals `1 Fire` damage on a hit.
