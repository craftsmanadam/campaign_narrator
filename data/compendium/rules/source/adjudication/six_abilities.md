# The Six Abilities

Every creature has six abilities:

- Strength: physical might
- Dexterity: agility, reflexes, and balance
- Constitution: health and stamina
- Intelligence: reasoning and memory
- Wisdom: perceptiveness and mental fortitude
- Charisma: confidence, poise, and charm

Ability scores normally range from 1 to 20 for adventurers, though monsters can reach 30.

- `1`: the lowest a score normally goes
- `2-9`: weak capability
- `10-11`: human average
- `12-19`: strong capability
- `20`: highest an adventurer normally reaches unless a feature says otherwise
- `21-29`: extraordinary capability
- `30`: highest possible score

Each ability also has a modifier applied to D20 Tests:

- `1`: `-5`
- `2-3`: `-4`
- `4-5`: `-3`
- `6-7`: `-2`
- `8-9`: `-1`
- `10-11`: `+0`
- `12-13`: `+1`
- `14-15`: `+2`
- `16-17`: `+3`
- `18-19`: `+4`
- `20-21`: `+5`
- `22-23`: `+6`
- `24-25`: `+7`
- `26-27`: `+8`
- `28-29`: `+9`
- `30`: `+10`

Whenever a rule divides or multiplies a number and produces a fraction, round down unless a rule explicitly says to round up.
