# Combat Actions

The first-cut prototype should support the universal combat actions most likely to appear in play and evaluation.

## Key Actions

- Attack
- Dash
- Dodge
- Help
- Hide
- Ready
- Search
- Utilize
- Magic
- Study

## Attack Basics

- Melee weapon and unarmed attacks usually use Strength.
- Ranged weapon attacks usually use Dexterity.
- Spell attacks use the spellcasting ability defined by the caster's feature.

## Light Weapon Rule

When you take the Attack action and attack with a Light weapon, you can make one extra attack later on the same turn with a different Light weapon. By default, you do not add the ability modifier to the extra attack's damage unless that modifier is negative.

## Readied and Reactive Play

- Ready sets up a later reaction.
- Reaction spells such as `Shield` and `Counterspell` depend on visible triggers.

## Search and Study

These actions matter for:

- trap and hidden-object detection,
- identifying illusions or ongoing spells,
- information-focused scenes.
