# Vision and Light

Some tasks rely on sight, including:

- noticing danger
- hitting an enemy
- targeting certain spells

Areas can be obscured:

- Lightly Obscured:
  - examples: Dim Light, patchy fog, moderate foliage
  - effect: Disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight
- Heavily Obscured:
  - examples: Darkness, heavy fog, dense foliage
  - effect: the area is opaque, and a creature is effectively Blinded when trying to see something there

Light categories:

- Bright Light:
  - most creatures see normally
  - examples: daylight, torches, lanterns, fires
- Dim Light:
  - creates a Lightly Obscured area
  - examples: twilight, dawn, some moonlight
- Darkness:
  - creates a Heavily Obscured area

Special senses that can change perception rules include:

- Blindsight
- Darkvision
- Tremorsense
- Truesight
