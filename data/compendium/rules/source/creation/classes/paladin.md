# Paladin

## Core Traits

- Primary ability: Strength and Charisma
- Hit Point Die: `d10`
- Saves: Wisdom, Charisma
- Skills: choose `2` from Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Persuasion, or Religion
- Armor: Light, Medium, Heavy, Shields
- Weapons: Simple, Martial
- Starting equipment: Chain Mail, Shield, Longsword, `6 Javelins`, Holy Symbol, Priest's Pack, and `9 GP`; or `150 GP`

## Level 1 to 5

- Level 1: `Lay On Hands`, `Spellcasting`, `Weapon Mastery`
- Level 2: `Fighting Style`, `Paladin's Smite`
- Level 3: `Channel Divinity`, Paladin subclass
- Level 4: feat or Ability Score Improvement
- Level 5: `Extra Attack`, `Faithful Steed`

## Spellcasting Shape

- Prepared level 1+ spells at level 1: 2
- At level 5, prepared spells: `6`
- Spellcasting ability: Charisma
- Can use a Holy Symbol as a spellcasting focus
- Spell slots progress as `2` level-1 slots at level 1, `3` level-1 at level 2, `4/2` at level 5.

## Key Mechanics

- `Lay On Hands` uses a pool of healing power equal to `5 x Paladin level`
- `Divine Sense` comes from Channel Divinity
- `Paladin's Smite` grants always-prepared `Divine Smite` and one free casting per Long Rest
- `Weapon Mastery` starts with `2` chosen weapon kinds
- `Channel Divinity` starts at level 3
  - You can use this class's Channel Divinity twice
  - regain one expended use on a Short Rest
  - regain all expended uses on a Long Rest
  - starts with `Divine Sense`
- `Aura of Protection` begins at level 6 and should be treated as a major future milestone

## Subclass in the SRD

`Oath of Devotion`

- Level 3: oath spells, `Sacred Weapon`
- Level 7: `Aura of Devotion`
- Level 15: `Smite of Protection`
- Level 20: `Holy Nimbus`

## First-Cut Use

The Paladin combines martial turns with burst spell use and healing. For the prototype, legal build support needs:

- Fighting Style choice,
- prepared spell list,
- Lay On Hands pool,
- and Channel Divinity use tracking.
