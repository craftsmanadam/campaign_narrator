# Cleric

## Core Traits

- Primary ability: Wisdom
- Hit Point Die: `d8`
- Saves: Wisdom, Charisma
- Skills: choose `2` from History, Insight, Medicine, Persuasion, or Religion
- Weapons: Simple
- Armor: Light, Medium, Shields
- Starting equipment: Chain Shirt, Shield, Mace, Holy Symbol, Priest's Pack, and `7 GP`; or `110 GP`

## Level 1 to 5

- Level 1: `Spellcasting`, `Divine Order`
- Level 2: `Channel Divinity`
- Level 3: Cleric subclass
- Level 4: feat or Ability Score Improvement
- Level 5: `Sear Undead`

## Spellcasting Shape

- Cantrips known at level 1: 3
- Prepared level 1+ spells at level 1: 4
- At level 3, prepared spells: `6`
- Spellcasting ability: Wisdom
- Can use a Holy Symbol as a spellcasting focus
- Spell slots progress as `2` level-1 slots at level 1, `3` level-1 at level 2, `4/2` at level 3, `4/3` at level 4, and `4/3/2` at level 5.

## Divine Order

Choose one:

- `Protector`: Martial weapons and Heavy armor
- `Thaumaturge`: one extra Cleric cantrip and a Wisdom-based bonus to Arcana or Religion

## Channel Divinity

Starts with:

- `Divine Spark`
- `Turn Undead`

You can use this class's Channel Divinity twice.

- regain one expended use on a Short Rest
- regain all expended uses on a Long Rest
- save DC uses the Cleric spell save DC
- `Divine Spark`: Magic action, creature within 30 feet, roll `1d8 + Wisdom modifier` to heal or deal Necrotic or Radiant damage; scales to `2d8` at level 7
- `Turn Undead`: Magic action, each chosen Undead within 30 feet makes a Wisdom save or becomes Frightened and Incapacitated for 1 minute, trying to move away

This is a major stateful class resource and matters directly for undead encounters and emergency healing.

## Subclass in the SRD

`Life Domain`

- Always-prepared domain spells starting at level 3
- Level 3: `Disciple of Life`, `Preserve Life`
  - `Disciple of Life`: healing from a spell slot gains `2 + spell slot level`
  - `Preserve Life`: spend Channel Divinity to restore hit points equal to `5 x Cleric level`, divided among Bloodied creatures within 30 feet, but no target can be restored above half HP
- Level 6: `Blessed Healer`
- Level 17: `Supreme Healing`

## First-Cut Use

The Cleric is one of the strongest support anchors for the prototype because it combines:

- prepared spell flexibility,
- reliable healing,
- anti-undead control,
- and subclass-driven domain identity.
