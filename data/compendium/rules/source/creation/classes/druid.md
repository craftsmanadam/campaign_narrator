# Druid

## Core Traits

- Primary ability: Wisdom
- Hit Point Die: `d8`
- Saves: Intelligence, Wisdom
- Skills: choose `2` from Animal Handling, Arcana, Insight, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Religion, or Survival
- Armor: Light, Shields
- Weapons: Simple
- Tool: Herbalism Kit
- Starting equipment: Leather Armor, Shield, Sickle, Druidic Focus (Quarterstaff), Explorer's Pack, Herbalism Kit, and `9 GP`; or `50 GP`

## Level 1 to 5

- Level 1: `Spellcasting`, `Druidic`, `Primal Order`
- Level 2: `Wild Shape`, `Wild Companion`
- Level 3: Druid subclass
- Level 4: feat or Ability Score Improvement
- Level 5: `Wild Resurgence`

## Spellcasting Shape

- Cantrips known at level 1: 2
- Prepared level 1+ spells at level 1: 4
- At level 3, prepared spells: `6`
- Spellcasting ability: Wisdom
- Can use a Druidic Focus as a spellcasting focus
- Spell slots progress as `2` level-1 slots at level 1, `3` level-1 at level 2, `4/2` at level 3, `4/3` at level 4, and `4/3/2` at level 5.

## Wild Shape

At level 2:

- starts with 2 uses,
- You know four Beast forms
- max CR `1/4`,
- no Fly Speed yet,
- grants Temporary Hit Points equal to your Druid level

While shape-shifted:

- keep mental ability scores,
- keep class features and proficiencies,
- cannot cast spells,
- keep Concentration on an existing spell.
- game statistics are otherwise replaced by the Beast stat block
- equipment falls, merges, or is worn depending on the form
- known forms increase to `6` and max CR `1/2` at level 4

## Subclass in the SRD

`Circle of the Land`

- Level 3: land-specific prepared spells, `Land's Aid`
- Level 6: `Natural Recovery`
- Level 10: `Nature's Ward`
- Level 14: `Nature's Sanctuary`

## First-Cut Use

The Druid needs structured support for:

- Wild Shape form selection,
- prepared spells,
- and land-type-dependent subclass spell packages.
