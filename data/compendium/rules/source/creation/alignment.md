# Alignment

Alignment is a shorthand for a character's moral compass. During character creation, choose an alignment and record it on the character sheet.

For this SRD subset, the main play assumption is that player characters are not evil-aligned. A player should check with the GM before creating an evil character.

Alignment is part of character identity rather than a numeric subsystem. It informs portrayal and decision-making but does not replace the game's other rules.
