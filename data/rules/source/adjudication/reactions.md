# Reactions

A Reaction is a special instant response to a trigger. The trigger can occur on the creature's turn or on someone else's turn.

Rules for Reactions:

- when a creature takes a Reaction, it cannot take another one until the start of its next turn
- if a Reaction interrupts another creature's turn, that creature continues its turn immediately after the Reaction unless the Reaction says otherwise
- a Reaction occurs immediately after its trigger unless the Reaction's description specifies different timing

Opportunity Attack is the most common Reaction type in normal play.
