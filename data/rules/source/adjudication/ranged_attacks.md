# Ranged Attacks

Ranged attacks include firing weapons, throwing weapons, and making ranged spell attacks.

Range rules:

- a ranged attack can target only within its specified range
- if a ranged attack has two range numbers, the first is normal range and the second is long range
- attacks beyond normal range but within long range are made with Disadvantage
- attacks beyond long range cannot be made

Ranged attacks in close combat:

- a ranged attack roll with a weapon, spell, or similar method has Disadvantage if the attacker is within 5 feet of an enemy
- that nearby enemy must be able to see the attacker
- that nearby enemy must not have the Incapacitated condition
