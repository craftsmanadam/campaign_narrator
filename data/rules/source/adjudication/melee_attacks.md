# Melee Attacks

Melee attacks usually involve handheld weapons, Unarmed Strikes, claws, bites, or other close-range attacks.

Reach:

- most creatures have 5-foot reach for melee attacks
- some creatures and attacks have a longer reach, as specified in their descriptions

Opportunity Attacks:

- a creature can make an Opportunity Attack when a creature it can see leaves its reach
- making the attack uses the creature's Reaction
- the Opportunity Attack is one melee attack with a weapon or an Unarmed Strike
- the attack occurs immediately before the target leaves reach

Avoiding Opportunity Attacks:

- taking the Disengage action prevents provoking them
- teleporting does not provoke them
- being moved without using movement, action, Bonus Action, or Reaction does not provoke them

Mounted combat and underwater combat are deferred and tracked in `docs/excluded_rules.md`, so they are intentionally not modeled in this file yet.
