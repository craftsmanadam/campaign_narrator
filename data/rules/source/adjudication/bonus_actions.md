# Bonus Actions

Some class features, spells, and other abilities let a creature take an additional action on its turn called a Bonus Action.

Rules for Bonus Actions:

- a creature can take a Bonus Action only when a rule explicitly grants one
- if no rule grants one, the creature has no Bonus Action to take
- a creature can take only one Bonus Action on its turn
- if multiple Bonus Actions are available, the creature chooses which one to use
- the creature chooses when to take the Bonus Action unless the rule that grants it specifies the timing
- anything that prevents the creature from taking actions also prevents it from taking a Bonus Action
