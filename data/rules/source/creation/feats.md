# Feats

Feats are organized in the SRD by category.

## Categories

- Origin Feats
- General Feats
- Fighting Style Feats
- Epic Boon Feats

The first-cut prototype includes the first three categories for normal play through level 5. Epic Boons remain excluded because they target level 19+ play.

## Feat Anatomy

Each feat can define:

- category,
- prerequisite,
- benefits,
- repeatability.

If a feat has a class prerequisite, the character must have at least one level in that class unless another rule says otherwise.

## Origin Feats in the SRD Slice

- Alert
  - adds Proficiency Bonus to Initiative
  - allows initiative swapping with a willing ally
- Magic Initiate
  - grants two cantrips and one level 1 spell from Cleric, Druid, or Wizard
  - the spell can be cast once per Long Rest without a slot
  - repeatable with a different spell list
- Savage Attacker
  - once per turn, reroll weapon damage dice and use either result
- Skilled
  - gain proficiency in any combination of three skills or tools
  - repeatable

## General and Fighting Style Examples Relevant by Level 5

- Ability Score Improvement
  - prerequisite: level `4+`
  - increase one ability score by `2`, or two ability scores by `1`
  - repeatable
- Grappler
  - prerequisite: level `4+`, Strength or Dexterity `13+`
  - increase Strength or Dexterity by `1`
  - `Punch and Grab`: once per turn, a hit with an Unarmed Strike as part of the Attack action can use both Damage and Grapple
  - Advantage on attack rolls against a creature grappled by you
  - no extra movement cost to move a grappled creature of your size or smaller
- Archery
  - prerequisite: Fighting Style feature
  - `+2` bonus to attack rolls with ranged weapons
- Defense
  - prerequisite: Fighting Style feature
  - `+1` Armor Class while wearing Light, Medium, or Heavy armor
- Great Weapon Fighting
  - prerequisite: Fighting Style feature
  - when rolling damage for a qualifying two-handed or versatile melee weapon, treat `1` or `2` on a damage die as `3`
- Two-Weapon Fighting
  - prerequisite: Fighting Style feature
  - add ability modifier to the damage of the extra Light-weapon attack if not already adding it

## Implementation Notes

- Backgrounds grant the initial Origin feat.
- Level-based feat acquisition later in play should check prerequisites and repeatability.
- Level 4 feat choice matters for the level 1 to 5 prototype and should be persisted explicitly.
- Epic Boon feats exist in the SRD feat chapter but remain excluded from first-cut play support.
