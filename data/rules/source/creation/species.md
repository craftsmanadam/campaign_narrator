# Species

All SRD species in this first cut are Humanoid. Species define creature type, size, speed, and special traits.

## Shared Structure

Each species entry provides:

- creature type,
- size,
- speed,
- special traits,
- and sometimes scalable features that improve at character levels 3 or 5.

All playable SRD species in this corpus are `Humanoid`. Most species normally live about 80 years unless a species entry says otherwise, and all reach physical maturity at about the same age.

## SRD Species Included in First Cut

### Dragonborn

- Medium, Speed 30
- Choose a draconic ancestry that sets damage type
- Breath Weapon replaces one attack in the Attack action
- Damage Resistance tied to ancestry
- Darkvision 60 feet
- Draconic Flight at level 5

### Dwarf

- Medium, Speed 30
- Darkvision 120 feet
- Resistance to poison damage
- Advantage against the Poisoned condition
- Dwarven Toughness increases max HP by 1 per level
- Stonecunning grants temporary Tremorsense on stone

### Elf

- Medium, Speed 30
- Darkvision 60 feet
- Choose Drow, High Elf, or Wood Elf lineage
- Gains lineage spells at levels 1, 3, and 5
- Fey Ancestry
- Skill choice from Insight, Perception, or Survival
- Trance allows a 4-hour long rest

### Gnome

- Small, Speed 30
- Darkvision 60 feet
- Gnomish Cunning grants Advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws
- Choose Forest or Rock lineage

### Goliath

- Medium, Speed 35
- Choose a Giant Ancestry benefit with PB-based uses
- Large Form at level 5
- Powerful Build

### Halfling

- Small, Speed 30
- Brave
- Halfling Nimbleness
- Luck
- Naturally Stealthy

### Human

- Medium or Small, Speed 30
- Resourceful grants Heroic Inspiration after a Long Rest
- One skill proficiency of choice
- One Origin feat of choice

### Orc

- Medium, Speed 30
- Darkvision 120 feet
- Adrenaline Rush
- Relentless Endurance

### Tiefling

- Medium or Small, Speed 30
- Darkvision 60 feet
- Fiendish Legacy with level 1, 3, and 5 spell progression
- Otherworldly Presence grants Thaumaturgy

## Scaling Traits to Remember

The first-cut prototype should specifically support species features that scale by level, including:

- Dragonborn Breath Weapon
- Dragonborn Draconic Flight
- Elf lineage spells
- Goliath Large Form
- Tiefling legacy spells

## Mechanical Details Worth Preserving Exactly

- Dragonborn Breath Weapon save DC is `8 + Constitution modifier + Proficiency Bonus`.
- Dragonborn Breath Weapon scales at character levels `5`, `11`, and `17`.
- Elf and Tiefling lineage or legacy spells are always prepared, can each be cast once without a spell slot per Long Rest, and can also be cast with spell slots.
- Gnome lineage spells use a chosen mental ability score: Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma.
- Goliath ancestry benefits use Proficiency Bonus-based uses per Long Rest.
- Orc `Adrenaline Rush` recharges on a Short or Long Rest.
