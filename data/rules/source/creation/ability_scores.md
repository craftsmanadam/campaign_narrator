# Ability Scores

The six abilities are Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma.

## What They Measure

- Strength: physical might
- Dexterity: agility, reflexes, and balance
- Constitution: health and stamina
- Intelligence: reasoning and memory
- Wisdom: perception and mental fortitude
- Charisma: confidence, poise, and charm

## Score Bands

- `1`: the lowest a score normally goes
- `2-9`: weak capability
- `10-11`: human average
- `12-19`: strong capability
- `20`: highest normal adventurer score unless a feature says otherwise
- `21-29`: extraordinary capability
- `30`: highest possible score

## Ability Modifiers

Ability modifiers are derived from the score and are used in D20 Tests. Key breakpoints:

- `8-9`: `-1`
- `10-11`: `+0`
- `12-13`: `+1`
- `14-15`: `+2`
- `16-17`: `+3`
- `18-19`: `+4`
- `20-21`: `+5`

## Background-Based Increases

Each SRD background lists three ability scores. Increase:

- one of those scores by 2 and another by 1, or
- all three by 1.

None of these increases can raise a score above 20.

## Rounding Rule

Whenever the game tells you to divide or multiply and you get a fraction, round down unless a rule explicitly says otherwise.
