# Sorcerer

## Core Traits

- Primary ability: Charisma
- Hit Point Die: `d6`
- Saves: Constitution, Charisma
- Skills: choose `2` from Arcana, Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Persuasion, or Religion
- Armor: none
- Weapons: Simple
- Starting equipment: Light Crossbow, `20 Bolts`, Arcane Focus, `2 Daggers`, Scholar's Pack, and `28 GP`; or `50 GP`

## Level 1 to 5

- Level 1: `Spellcasting`, `Innate Sorcery`
- Level 2: `Font of Magic`, `Metamagic`
- Level 3: Sorcerer subclass
- Level 4: feat or Ability Score Improvement
- Level 5: `Sorcerous Restoration`

## Spellcasting Shape

- Cantrips known at level 1: 4
- Prepared level 1+ spells at level 1: 2
- At level 3, prepared spells: `6`
- Spellcasting ability: Charisma
- Can use an Arcane Focus as a spellcasting focus
- Spell slots progress as `2` level-1 slots at level 1, `3` level-1 at level 2, `4/2` at level 3, `4/3` at level 4, and `4/3/2` at level 5.

## Core Sorcerer Resource Loop

- `Innate Sorcery` boosts Sorcerer spell save DC and Sorcerer spell attack rolls
- `Sorcery Points` can convert to and from spell slots
- `Metamagic` modifies spells at cast time

### Resource Details

- `Innate Sorcery`: Bonus Action, lasts `1 minute`, usable twice per Long Rest; while active your Sorcerer spell save DC increases by `1` and Sorcerer spell attack rolls have Advantage
- At level 2, Sorcery Points: `2`
- At level 3, Sorcery Points: `3`
- At level 5, Sorcery Points: `5`
- `Metamagic` begins with two options at level 2
- `Sorcerous Restoration` at level 5 restores Sorcery Points on a Short Rest equal to half Sorcerer level, rounded down, once per Long Rest

Important first-cut Metamagic options include:

- `Quickened Spell`
- `Twinned Spell`
- `Careful Spell`
- `Subtle Spell`

## Subclass in the SRD

`Draconic Sorcery`

- Level 3: `Draconic Resilience`, `Draconic Spells`
- later features continue the dragon-themed progression

## First-Cut Use

The Sorcerer matters because it introduces the prototype's first strong example of spell-shape modification. The system should persist:

- Sorcery Points,
- known Metamagic options,
- and whether `Innate Sorcery` is active.
