# Sorcerer

## Core Traits

- Primary ability: Charisma
- Hit Point Die: `d6`
- Saves: Constitution, Charisma
- Armor: none
- Weapons: Simple

## Level 1 to 5

- Level 1: `Spellcasting`, `Innate Sorcery`
- Level 2: `Font of Magic`, `Metamagic`
- Level 3: Sorcerer subclass
- Level 4: feat or Ability Score Improvement
- Level 5: `Sorcerous Restoration`

## Spellcasting Shape

- Cantrips known at level 1: 4
- Prepared level 1+ spells at level 1: 2
- Spellcasting ability: Charisma
- Can use an Arcane Focus as a spellcasting focus

## Core Sorcerer Resource Loop

- `Innate Sorcery` boosts Sorcerer spell save DC and Sorcerer spell attack rolls
- `Sorcery Points` can convert to and from spell slots
- `Metamagic` modifies spells at cast time

Important first-cut Metamagic options include:

- `Quickened Spell`
- `Twinned Spell`
- `Careful Spell`
- `Subtle Spell`

## Subclass in the SRD

`Draconic Sorcery`

- Level 3: `Draconic Resilience`, `Draconic Spells`
- later features continue the dragon-themed progression

## First-Cut Use

The Sorcerer matters because it introduces the prototype's first strong example of spell-shape modification. The system should persist:

- Sorcery Points,
- known Metamagic options,
- and whether `Innate Sorcery` is active.
