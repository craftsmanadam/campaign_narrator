# Warlock

## Core Traits

- Primary ability: Charisma
- Hit Point Die: `d8`
- Saves: Wisdom, Charisma
- Skills: choose `2` from Arcana, Deception, History, Intimidation, Investigation, Nature, or Religion
- Armor: Light
- Weapons: Simple
- Starting equipment: Leather Armor, Sickle, `2 Daggers`, Arcane Focus (orb), Book (occult lore), Scholar's Pack, and `15 GP`; or `100 GP`

## Level 1 to 5

- Level 1: `Eldritch Invocations`, `Pact Magic`
- Level 2: `Magical Cunning`
- Level 3: Warlock subclass
- Level 4: feat or Ability Score Improvement
- Level 5: stronger Pact Magic slots and invocation access

### Invocation Progression

- At level 1, invocations: `1`
- At level 2, invocations: `3`
- At level 5, invocations: `5`

## Pact Magic

Warlock casting differs from standard Spellcasting:

- At level 1, Pact Magic slots: `1`
- At level 1, Pact Magic slot level: `1`
- At level 3, Pact Magic slot level: `2`
- At level 5, Pact Magic slot level: `3`
- all Pact Magic slots are the same level,
- slots recharge on a Short or Long Rest,
- prepared spells start at `2` and rise to `4` at level 3 and `6` at level 5,
- cantrips start at `2`, increase to `3` at level 4,
- spellcasting ability is Charisma,
- Arcane Focus works as spellcasting focus.

## Magical Cunning

- 1-minute rite
- regain expended Pact Magic spell slots up to half your maximum, rounded up
- usable once per Long Rest

## Eldritch Invocations

Invocations are a major class-defining choice. Important first-cut examples:

- `Agonizing Blast`
- `Devil's Sight`
- `Armor of Shadows`
- `Pact of the Blade`
- `Pact of the Chain`
- `Pact of the Tome`
- `Thirsting Blade`

Invocation prerequisites matter. A Warlock can replace one invocation on each Warlock level-up, but cannot replace an invocation that is serving as a prerequisite for another invocation they still have.

## Subclass in the SRD

`Fiend Patron`

- Level 3: `Dark One's Blessing`, `Fiend Spells`
  - `Dark One's Blessing`: when you reduce an enemy to `0 HP`, or an enemy within `10 feet` is reduced to `0 HP` by someone else, gain Temporary Hit Points equal to `Charisma modifier + Warlock level`, minimum `1`
  - `Fiend Spells`: always-prepared spells at Warlock level 3 are `Burning Hands`, `Command`, `Scorching Ray`, and `Suggestion`
- Level 6: `Dark One's Own Luck`
- Level 10: `Fiendish Resilience`
- Level 14: `Hurl Through Hell`

## First-Cut Use

The Warlock is important because it forces the system to distinguish:

- Pact Magic from normal Spellcasting,
- invocation-based permanent choices,
- and patron-driven always-prepared spell sets.
