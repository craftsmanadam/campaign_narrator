# Barbarian

## Core Traits

- Primary ability: Strength
- Hit Point Die: `d12`
- Saves: Strength, Constitution
- Skills: choose `2` from Animal Handling, Athletics, Intimidation, Nature, Perception, or Survival
- Armor: Light, Medium, Shields
- Weapons: Simple, Martial
- Starting equipment: `Greataxe`, `4 Handaxes`, Explorer's Pack, and `15 GP`; or `75 GP`

## Level 1 to 5

- Level 1: `Rage`, `Unarmored Defense`, `Weapon Mastery`
- Level 2: `Danger Sense`, `Reckless Attack`
- Level 3: Barbarian subclass, `Primal Knowledge`
- Level 4: feat or Ability Score Improvement
- Level 5: `Extra Attack`, `Fast Movement`

## Key Mechanics

### Rage

- At level 1, Rage uses: `2`
- At level 1, Rage damage bonus: `+2`
- Rage lasts until the end of your next turn.
- Rage can be extended by making an attack roll against an enemy, forcing an enemy to make a saving throw, or taking a Bonus Action.
- Rage lasts for up to `10 minutes`.
- While active, Rage grants Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.
- While active, Rage gives Advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.
- While active, the Barbarian cannot maintain Concentration and cannot cast spells.
- Rage ends early if you don Heavy armor or have the Incapacitated condition.

### Other Low-Level Features

- `Unarmored Defense`: base AC is `10 + Dexterity modifier + Constitution modifier` while unarmored; Shields still work.
- `Weapon Mastery`: starts with `2` chosen Simple or Martial Melee weapon kinds; increases to `3` at level 4.
- `Danger Sense`: Advantage on Dexterity saving throws unless you have the Incapacitated condition.
- `Reckless Attack`: on your first attack roll of the turn, you can gain Advantage on Strength-based attack rolls until your next turn, but attack rolls against you have Advantage.
- `Primal Knowledge`: gain one Barbarian skill proficiency, and while raging you can use Strength for Acrobatics, Intimidation, Perception, Stealth, or Survival checks.
- `Fast Movement`: at level 5 your Speed increases by `10 feet` while you are not wearing Heavy armor.

## Subclass in the SRD

`Path of the Berserker`

- Level 3: `Frenzy`
  - while raging and using Reckless Attack, the first Strength-based hit on your turn deals extra damage equal to a number of `d6` equal to your Rage Damage bonus
- Level 6: `Mindless Rage`
- Level 10: `Retaliation`
- Level 14: `Intimidating Presence`

## First-Cut Use

For the prototype, treat the Barbarian as a front-line martial with simple resource tracking:

- Rage uses,
- rage duration,
- weapon mastery choices,
- and Reckless Attack state.
