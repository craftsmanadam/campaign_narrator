# Bard

## Core Traits

- Primary ability: Charisma
- Hit Point Die: `d8`
- Saves: Dexterity, Charisma
- Armor: Light
- Weapons: Simple
- Tools: three Musical Instruments
- Skills: choose any three

## Level 1 to 5

- Level 1: `Bardic Inspiration`, `Spellcasting`
- Level 2: `Expertise`, `Jack of All Trades`
- Level 3: Bard subclass
- Level 4: feat or Ability Score Improvement
- Level 5: `Font of Inspiration`

## Spellcasting Shape

- Cantrips known at level 1: 2
- Prepared level 1+ spells at level 1: 4
- Spellcasting ability: Charisma
- Can use a Musical Instrument as a spellcasting focus

Recommended early spells and cantrips in the SRD include:

- `Dancing Lights`
- `Vicious Mockery`
- `Charm Person`
- `Dissonant Whispers`
- `Healing Word`

## Bardic Inspiration

- Starts as a `d6`
- Granted as a Bonus Action to a creature within 60 feet that can see or hear the Bard
- Used when that creature fails a D20 Test within the next hour
- At level 5, all uses recharge on a Short or Long Rest

## Subclass in the SRD

`College of Lore`

- Level 3: `Bonus Proficiencies`, `Cutting Words`
- Level 6: `Magical Discoveries`
- Level 14: `Peerless Skill`

## First-Cut Use

The Bard should be modeled as a support caster with:

- dynamic prepared spell list,
- inspiration die tracking,
- and strong social-skill interaction support.
