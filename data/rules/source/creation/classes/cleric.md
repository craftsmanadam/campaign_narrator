# Cleric

## Core Traits

- Primary ability: Wisdom
- Hit Point Die: `d8`
- Saves: Wisdom, Charisma
- Armor and weapons depend partly on `Divine Order`

## Level 1 to 5

- Level 1: `Spellcasting`, `Divine Order`
- Level 2: `Channel Divinity`
- Level 3: Cleric subclass
- Level 4: feat or Ability Score Improvement
- Level 5: `Sear Undead`

## Spellcasting Shape

- Cantrips known at level 1: 3
- Prepared level 1+ spells at level 1: 4
- Spellcasting ability: Wisdom
- Can use a Holy Symbol as a spellcasting focus

## Divine Order

Choose one:

- `Protector`: Martial weapons and Heavy armor
- `Thaumaturge`: one extra Cleric cantrip and a Wisdom-based bonus to Arcana or Religion

## Channel Divinity

Starts with:

- `Divine Spark`
- `Turn Undead`

This is a major stateful class resource and matters directly for undead encounters and emergency healing.

## Subclass in the SRD

`Life Domain`

- Always-prepared domain spells starting at level 3
- Level 3: `Disciple of Life`, `Preserve Life`
- Level 6: `Blessed Healer`
- Level 17: `Supreme Healing`

## First-Cut Use

The Cleric is one of the strongest support anchors for the prototype because it combines:

- prepared spell flexibility,
- reliable healing,
- anti-undead control,
- and subclass-driven domain identity.
