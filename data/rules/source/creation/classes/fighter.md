# Fighter

## Core Traits

- Primary ability: Strength or Dexterity
- Hit Point Die: `d10`
- Saves: Strength, Constitution
- Armor: Light, Medium, Heavy, Shields
- Weapons: Simple, Martial

## Level 1 to 5

- Level 1: `Fighting Style`, `Second Wind`, `Weapon Mastery`
- Level 2: `Action Surge`, `Tactical Mind`
- Level 3: Fighter subclass
- Level 4: feat or Ability Score Improvement
- Level 5: `Extra Attack`, `Tactical Shift`

## Key Mechanics

- `Second Wind` is a Bonus Action self-heal with multiple uses over the adventuring day.
- `Action Surge` grants an extra action, except the Magic action.
- `Weapon Mastery` starts with three weapon kinds at level 1.

## Subclass in the SRD

`Champion`

- Level 3: `Improved Critical`, `Remarkable Athlete`
- Level 7: `Additional Fighting Style`
- Level 10: `Heroic Warrior`
- Level 15: `Superior Critical`
- Level 18: `Survivor`

## First-Cut Use

The Fighter is the simplest martial baseline for the prototype. The important things to persist are:

- Fighting Style choice,
- Second Wind uses,
- Action Surge availability,
- weapon mastery choices,
- and subclass crit range changes for Champion.
