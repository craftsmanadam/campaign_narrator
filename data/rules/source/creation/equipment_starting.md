# Equipment and Starting Gear

This file summarizes the equipment rules most relevant to character creation and early play.

## Money

Coin values relative to 1 GP:

- CP: `1/100 GP`
- SP: `1/10 GP`
- EP: `1/2 GP`
- GP: `1 GP`
- PP: `10 GP`

Fifty coins weigh one pound.

## Starting Equipment from Backgrounds

Each SRD background offers:

- one predefined equipment package, or
- `50 GP`.

## Weapon Basics for Character Creation

Weapons are grouped as:

- Simple or Martial
- Melee or Ranged

Weapon entries provide:

- damage die and type,
- properties,
- mastery property,
- weight,
- cost.

Important properties for the first cut:

- Ammunition
- Finesse
- Heavy
- Light
- Loading
- Range
- Reach
- Thrown
- Two-Handed
- Versatile

Important mastery properties for the first cut:

- Cleave
- Graze
- Nick
- Push
- Sap
- Slow
- Topple
- Vex

## Armor Basics for Character Creation

Armor categories:

- Light
- Medium
- Heavy
- Shield

Important rules:

- You need training to use armor or shields effectively.
- Without training, Strength and Dexterity D20 Tests are made with Disadvantage and you cannot cast spells while wearing Light, Medium, or Heavy armor.
- A creature can wear only one suit of armor and wield only one shield at a time.

## Tool Proficiencies from Backgrounds

Backgrounds grant one tool proficiency, often one of:

- Calligrapher's Supplies
- Thieves' Tools
- Gaming Set

Tools matter for later crafting and noncombat checks, so they should be persisted during character creation.
